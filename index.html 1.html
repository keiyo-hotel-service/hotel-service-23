About Keiyo Hotel Service

Keiyo Hotel Service Co., Ltd. is a dedicated company focused on professional cleaning and comprehensive building maintenance. Since our establishment in 1978, we have been committed to delivering high-quality sanitation services.

Our primary area of operation is Narita City, Chiba Prefecture, specializing in keeping local premium hotels in pristine condition. We prioritize reliability, efficiency, and cleanliness in every job.

Under the leadership of Representative Sanae Hasegawa, we have built a team that takes pride in ensuring guest satisfaction through immaculate room presentation. We are currently expanding and looking for dedicated individuals to join our professional maintenance team.
